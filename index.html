Saftey

Although many people believe that drones are not safe, they are very safe because of the built in precaution that they have. Most drone have an auto piolet mode just in case there is an emergency. For example if it is low on battery, there it will auto land itself on the ground safely.

Privacy

There are regulation and laws that you have to follow when you own a drone. It also has to be registered to the FFA.There are also common laws on flying drones.The US Supreme Court has held that individuals do not generally have Fourth Amendment rights with respect to aerial surveillance because of the ability that anyone might have to observe what could be viewed from the air.

Available Tech

Some technologies available for the drones are mountable cameras. There are sports camera company, called GoPro, have created small portable cameras that attach to the drone to take video. This video can be seen on any smartphone.

Future Advances

Some people would call this decade the age of the drone. It is expected by 2020 that there are at least 30,000 drones, owned by the average consumer and for government uses, flying at any point in time for a multitude of purposes.
