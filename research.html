Research Uses of Drones

By: TSA

Posted on January 31st, 2016 at 3:30 PM

Drones can be used for research into nuclear accidents. Equipment can be attached to drones that allow them to monitor the radiation level of the surrounding area. They can be used in glaciers to measure the melting of the ice caps. This information could then be used to evacuate the area before an avalanche occurs.

They can be flown over volcanoes to capture footage and gather information on lava flow and eruptions.

Nuclear accidents – measuring contamination.

Forestry – monitoring tree disease.
