Educational Uses of Drones

Posted on January 31st, 2016 at 3:30 PM

Drones allow students to gather information and data previously unaccessible for most people. Examples include mapping the flow of lava from volcanoes, surveying forest canopies, and monitoring wildlife.

They can be used for creating promotional videos and walkthroughs of colleges and universities

Schools in our area are actively pursuing interest in drones. Conestoga Valley High School recently purchased two drones for use in video production and physics classes.
