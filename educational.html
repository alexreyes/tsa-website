Educational Uses of Drones

By: TSA

Posted on January 31st, 2016 at 3:30 PM

Drones serve many different purposes and can be modified to fit a variety of use cases. The educational market has taken an interest in the newly growing drone market. Conestoga Valley High School recently purchased two drones for use in its video production classes. The drones allow the class to capture footage from sporting events and extracurricular events. The footage could then be edited for broadcasting on the school’s news network to highlight particular plays. They could be used in gym classes to show how a particular skill could be improved, such as basketball free throw shots. Drones will only increase in popularity amongst schools as the technology becomes cheaper and more accessible. As computer technology progresses, schools can go longer without having to replace old equipment, which allows them to allocate money into extra programs, such as buying drones. High Schools may take up an interest in adding aeronautics classes as student’s purchase their own drones for personal use. In the future it won’t be uncommon to have students bring their own drones to school to work on projects.
